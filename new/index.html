Since 2019, the Texts Surrounding Texts project has digitized and catalogued hundreds of Tamil and Sanskrit manuscripts from the Bibliothèque nationale de France (BnF) and the Staats- und Universitätsbibliothek Hamburg (SUB), focusing particularly on South Indian palm-leaf manuscripts.

Collections

See digitized manuscripts from the BnF and the SUB, as well as selected manuscripts from archives around the world.

Paratexts

Paratexts — such as marginal blessings, scribal colophons, and ownership statements — are one of the primary research topics of TST.

Palæography

Tamil manuscripts exhibit a number of ligatures and special signs that were never used in printed books.

Tools

Through the course of the project, a number of digital tools have been developed for describing and cataloguing Indian manuscripts.

Blog

Read about highlights of the collections and keep up to date with new discoveries.

Texts Surrounding Texts: Satellite stanzas, prefaces, & colophons in South Indian manuscripts (BnF & Hamburg Stabi collections) was funded through a Franco-German collaboration in the Humanities and Social Sciences grant (FRAL 2018, ANR/DFG) from 2019–2023.
