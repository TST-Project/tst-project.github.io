SUB manuscripts

At the Staats- und Universitätsbibliothek Hamburg,, the majority of the Indian materials are shelved under Cod. Palmbl., denoting that they are palm-leaf manuscripts. There are also a number of items in the Cod. Orient. collection, as well as the Oppert and Schrader collections.

Texts Surrounding Texts: Satellite stanzas, prefaces, & colophons in South Indian manuscripts (BnF & Hamburg Stabi collections) was funded through a Franco-German collaboration in the Humanities and Social Sciences grant (FRAL 2018, ANR/DFG) from 2019–2023.
